# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** September 26, 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how MyKoty ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you use our services, including AlgoLens. We are committed to transparency and minimal data collection. This policy applies to all users of our services.

## 1. Information We Collect
- **Account Information:** Email address and username when you create an account.  
- **Usage Data:** Information such as pages visited, features used, browser, and operating system.  
- **Payment Information:** Processed securely by third-party providers. We do not store credit card details.  
- **Communications:** Messages or inquiries you send to us.  

We do not collect sensitive personal information unless you provide it voluntarily.

## 2. How We Use Your Information
We use the information we collect to:  
- Provide, maintain, and improve our services.  
- Process transactions and manage user accounts.  
- Respond to inquiries and support requests.  
- Send service updates or promotional messages (only with your consent).  
- Protect the security and integrity of the Service.  
- Comply with legal obligations.  

## 3. Sharing Your Information
We do **not** sell or rent your personal data. Information may be shared only:  
- With trusted service providers (e.g., hosting and payment processors) under confidentiality agreements.  
- When required by law or legal process.  
- In connection with a business transfer such as a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets.  

## 4. Data Retention & Your Rights
We retain personal data only as long as necessary to provide services or comply with legal obligations.  
You may request at any time:  
- **Access & Correction** of your personal data.  
- **Deletion** of your personal data.  
- **Opt-Out** of marketing communications.  
- **Data Portability** where technically feasible.  

Requests can be made by contacting us at the email below.

## 5. Data Security
We implement industry-standard safeguards such as encryption, secure servers, and periodic audits. However, no system can be guaranteed 100% secure.

## 6. Changes to This Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Significant changes will be announced on our website or communicated by email. Continued use of the Service after updates constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
